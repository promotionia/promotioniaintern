The Need for Talented Freshers

As Promotionia continues to grow and expand its operations, there is an increasing demand for fresh talent to join the team and contribute to the company's success. Recognizing the potential in nurturing young minds, Promotionia has decided to launch an internship program specifically aimed at college students. This program is designed not only to provide valuable work experience but also to identify and develop future leaders in the marketing industry.All positions are work from home.

Internship Process Overview

The internship selection process at Promotionia is rigorous and multi-faceted, ensuring that only the most dedicated and talented individuals are chosen. The process is divided into three main rounds:

First Round: Application and Resume Submission

Interested students are required to fill out a Google form which will serve as their initial application and resume submission. Each application will be carefully reviewed, and students will be notified via email whether they have been shortlisted for the next round or not. Successful candidates will receive a congratulatory email, while those who do not make it will be encouraged to try again in the future with a "better luck next time" message.

Apply Now

Second Round: Training and Task Assignment

Shortlisted candidates will undergo a brief training session lasting 1-2 hours. This training is designed to provide them with an overview of Promotionia's work culture, the basics of digital marketing, and the specific tasks they will be required to perform. Following the training, candidates will be given a task to complete over the course of 1-2 weeks. This task will allow them to demonstrate their skills and creativity in a real-world context, proving their capability to handle the demands of the job.

Third Round: Interview

Candidates who successfully complete the task will be invited to a final interview. This interview will focus on their learnings from the training and the task, as well as their understanding of basic marketing psychology and aptitude. The panel will ask questions related to the candidates' experiences during the training and task period, evaluating their problem-solving abilities, creativity, and overall fit for the company.

Internship Details

Students who pass all three rounds will be offered a confirmed internship at Promotionia. This internship will last for two months and come with a stipend ranging from 7,000 to 12,000, depending on the intern's performance during the selection process and throughout the internship period. Additionally, upon successful completion of the internship, students will receive a certificate recognizing their contributions and learnings.

Benefits of Internship In Promotionia

  • Real-World Experience: Interns will gain hands-on experience working on live projects for actual clients, allowing them to apply theoretical knowledge in practical scenarios.
  • Skill Development: Through training and task assignments, interns will develop essential skills in digital marketing, social media management, content creation, and more.
  • Mentorship: Interns will have the opportunity to learn from experienced professionals in the industry, receiving guidance and feedback to help them grow.
  • Networking: Working at Promotionia will enable interns to build a professional network, connecting with peers and industry leaders who can provide valuable career support.
  • Career Opportunities: Successful interns may be considered for full-time positions at Promotionia, paving the way for a promising career in marketing and advertising.
